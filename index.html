Welcome to Mr Burger

The best Burger n' Fries Shop in Vancouver, BC

Mr. Burger crafts a culinary haven with handcrafted, locally sourced burgers. Each bite is a tale of flavor, from juicy Angus beef to gourmet toppings. Customization reigns supreme, offering diverse options for every palate. Beyond food, it's a community pillar, fostering a shared love for exceptional burgers and local support.

About Us

In Burnaby, British Columbia, Mr. Burger sizzles as a burger haven, crafting juicy delights that ignite taste buds. Born from local food enthusiasts' dreams, it sources farm-fresh ingredients, elevating each bite into a flavorful journey. The "Burnaby Bliss," a signature dish, boasts a succulent beef patty, smoked cheddar, crispy bacon, and a secret tangy sauce hugged by a toasted brioche bun.

Amid vintage decor and vibrant tunes, Mr. Burger's ambiance matches its menu—inviting and community-driven. Every visit creates memorable moments, uniting locals and tourists alike in a shared love for these gourmet creations. From its humble beginnings to becoming Burnaby's talk, Mr. Burger leaves an indelible mark on every burger aficionado's palate.
