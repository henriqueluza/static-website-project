Our History

The origin of your favorite burger shop

In a culinary crescendo, Henrique Luza and Rafael Dias coalesced their gastronomic dreams, birthing Mr. Burger in 2005. The dynamic duo fused expertise, Luza’s heritage recipes entwining with Dias’s avant-garde culinary techniques. Their first humble eatery burgeoned into a sensation, boasting lines snaking around corners. The ethos of Mr. Burger transcended its delectable offerings. Luza and Dias championed sustainability, sourcing local produce and pioneering eco-friendly practices within the industry. Their symbiotic blend of tradition and innovation propelled the franchise into international acclaim. Today, Mr. Burger stands as an emblem of Luza and Dias’s shared passion, a testament to their flavorful partnership.

Our Ingredients

Where the flavour comes from?

The genesis of Mr. Burger’s signature products harks back to the culinary alchemy of Henrique Luza and Rafael Dias. Their quest for perfection birthed a saga of taste, sourcing prime beef cuts from local ranches blessed by lush pastures. Each patty was a canvas, seasoned with ancestral spices passed down through generations. The buns, a testament to meticulous crafting, arose from artisanal bakeries, blending tradition with contemporary flourishes. Handpicked vegetables graced the burgers, harvested at their peak to ensure unparalleled freshness. Mr. Burger’s secret sauces, a guarded legacy, were concocted in the midnight cauldron of creativity, intertwining flavors that danced on the palate. Infused with passion, every element bore the essence of dedication, nurturing a gastronomic legacy that tantalized taste buds and transcended mere meals to become flavorful memories etched in time.

FAQ

Some of your most asked questions and their answers

The founders, Henrique Luza and Rafael Dias, were driven by a shared passion for redefining burger culture. They sought to blend Luza's heritage recipes with Dias's innovative culinary techniques, creating a unique flavor profile. Their approach emphasized quality ingredients and a fusion of tradition with modern taste, setting Mr. Burger apart in the industry.

Mr. Burger's ingredients are carefully sourced from local ranches and trusted suppliers known for their high-quality produce. The company maintains close relationships with these partners, ensuring freshness and quality. For signature dishes, such as their renowned patties, prime beef cuts are handpicked from ranches known for ethical and sustainable practices, while vegetables are harvested at peak ripeness to guarantee flavor and freshness.

What distinguishes Mr. Burger is its commitment to community and sustainability. The company actively engages in supporting local initiatives and events, fostering a sense of belonging and giving back to the neighborhoods it serves. Moreover, Mr. Burger prioritizes sustainability by sourcing locally, reducing its carbon footprint, and implementing eco-friendly practices in its operations, aligning its success with environmental responsibility.
